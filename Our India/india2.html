Our India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Place - Agra

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi. It served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors for nearly 200 years, until 1856.

Place - New Delhi

India Gate

India Gate is a historic monument located in New Delhi, India's capital. It was designed to pay tribute to the soldiers who bravely battled during the First World War.

Place - New Delhi

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple, located in New Delhi, India, is a Baháʼí House of Worship that was dedicated in December 1986.

Place - New Delhi

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is a five-storey building, and it is the tallest building in the world that has been built without a foundation.

Place - Jaipur

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is the world's tallest minaret made of brick. Its height is 72.5 meters and its construction was initiated by Qutub-Ud-Din Aibak.

Place - New Delhi

Sanchi Stupa

The Sanchi Stupa is located in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. It is one of the most ancient and famous Buddhist monuments in India.

Place - Sanchi

Charminar

Charminar is one of the oldest monuments in India, and it is situated in Hyderabad, Telangana. Charminar was built in 1591 and is a symbol of Hyderabad.

Place - Hyderabad

Gateway Of India

The Gateway of India is an arch-monument completed in 1924 on the waterfront of Mumbai (Bombay), India.

Place - Mumbai

Jal Mahal

Jal Mahal is a palace in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur city, the capital of the state of Rajasthan, India.

Place - Jaipur
